Description

This app offers two modes:

  1. Custom Input: Enter up to three products to get tailored recommendations via co-purchase and semantic similarity.
  2. Surprise Me: Select a category and three products for a fun, randomized gift suggestion experience.

Use this tool to explore how AI and purchase behavior combine to deliver thoughtful gift ideas.
